This is a jam session for musicians of all levels and styles preceded by a short opening concert.

Location: MKZ Musikzentrum Kanzlei - Kanzleistrasse 56 8004 Zurich, rooms 1 & 2 (ground floor)
Door opening: 5.45 p.m.
Start: 6.00 p.m. Free admission / collection

We can play the following classics if during the jam session we run out of ideas: Autumn Leaves (G-), Body And Soul (Db), Bye Bye Blackbird (F), Chameleon 1 (Bb-), Days Of Wine And Roses (F), Fly Me To The Moon (C), Freddie Freeloader (Bb), How High The Moon (G), Memphis Underground (C), Mr. Magic (C-), On Green Dolphin Street (Eb), Red Baron (G-), Stella By Starlight (Bb), Stratus (not yet in the iRealPro playlist), There Will Never Be Another You (Eb).

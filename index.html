Past Jam Sessions

This is a jam session for musicians of all levels and styles preceded by a short opening concert.

Location: MKZ Musikzentrum Kanzlei - Kanzleistrasse 56 8004 Zurich, rooms 1 & 2 (ground floor)
Door opening: 5.45 p.m.
Start: 6.00 p.m. Free admission / collection

The jam sessions are mostly streamed live, either here youtube stream or here twitch stream, depending on who is streaming. So if you really can't show up in person don't forget to tune in on Sundays at 6pm!

We can play the following classics if during the jam session we run out of ideas: Autumn Leaves (G-), Body And Soul (Db), Bye Bye Blackbird (F), Chameleon 1 (Bb-), Days Of Wine And Roses (F), Fly Me To The Moon (C), Freddie Freeloader (Bb), How High The Moon (G), Memphis Underground (C), Mr. Magic (C-), On Green Dolphin Street (Eb), Red Baron (G-), Stella By Starlight (Bb), Stratus (not yet in the iRealPro playlist), There Will Never Be Another You (Eb). Get them as an iRealPro playlist!

We follow the usual jam session custom of max 2 songs per performer until all performers in the room have been called on stage, with an exception for rhythm section members who can stay longer on stage if nobody can take up their role.
